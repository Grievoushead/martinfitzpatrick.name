Homogenization Buffer





#Requirements
300 ml PM buffer
52.3 mg PMSF (1mM PMSF)
3 g leupeptin (10 mg/ml leupeptin)
300 mg pepstatin A (1 ug/ml pepstatin A)
3 mg TAME (10 ug/ml TAME) 

#Method

Combine ingredients in suitable container. Mix well to dissolve.


>Use immediately.
